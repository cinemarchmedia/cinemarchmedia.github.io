CineMarch Media, LLC

Brand Summary

CineMarch Media, LLC is a multimedia company producing socially conscious, critically engaged content across multiple platforms.

The company operates under the umbrella tagline:

"Revolutionary Ideas In Motion."

Mission Statement

CineMarch Media, LLC exists to create, amplify, and protect revolutionary storytelling.

We produce media that challenges dominant narratives, re-centers marginalized voices, and reimagines what art can do in a world that demands better.

Through film, performance, and critical discourse, we connect creators and audiences who believe that culture is not just entertainment ‚Äî it is a battleground for truth, memory, and liberation.

CineMarch Media is committed to building ethical, cooperative platforms that serve people, not profit. We stand for independent creation, radical honesty, artistic labor, and collective ownership.

We are more than a media company.

We are a movement in motion.
